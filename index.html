About Haybee Trade

Founded by Ajani Joseph Abayomi, Haybee Trade is committed to connecting businesses and individuals with high-quality products and reliable services. With a passion for excellence and a drive for innovation, Joseph established Haybee Trade to bridge the gap between suppliers and customers, ensuring satisfaction at every step.

  • Expand our network of trusted suppliers and clients
  • Continuously improve our services through innovation
  • Promote ethical business practices and sustainability

Our Services

  • Wholesale Distribution: Supplying products in bulk to businesses
  • Logistics Support: Ensuring timely and safe delivery of goods
